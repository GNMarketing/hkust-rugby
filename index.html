The Typhoons Club

Founded in 1991, the HKUST Typhoons Rugby Club proudly represents the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in university and local competitions.

Our team embodies rugby values: respect, solidarity, discipline, and self-improvement. We welcome players of all levels, from beginners to experienced athletes.
